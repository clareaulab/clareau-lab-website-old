Computational and Translational Immunology

The overall theme of our lab’s research is Computational and Translational Immunology, which

Our laboratory has broad interests in studying processes of somatic evolution whereby cells adapt, expand, differentiate, or die in response to genetic and environmental factors within an individual’s lifetime. We’re particularly interested in studying these processes in the hematopoetic system, spanning circulating blood to immune cells that are resident in tissues and tumors.

Research areas

Cancer immunotherapies

Mitochondrial genetics
Single-cell technologies
Somatic evolution
