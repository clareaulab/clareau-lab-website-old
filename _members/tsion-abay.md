### About
Tsion is currently a PhD student in the Biological and Biomedical Sciences program at Harvard University. 
Before starting graduate school, she worked as a Research Associate II working with Caleb and Bob Stickels in the Satpathy Lab
at the Gladstone Institutes. During her time in the group, she lead the development of 
new genomics technologies to study rare cells via single-cell sequencing. Tsion is a
graduate of Rowan University and was a Research Associate at Twist Biosciences.
